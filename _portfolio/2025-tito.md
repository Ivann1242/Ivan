Research with Dr. Grant Barkley at the University of Michigan (Sep 2025 – Apr 2026).

**Highlights:**
* Formalized order-comparability through inversion sets and proved correctness.
* Translated the theoretical model into a computable structure for sequence comparison and ranking.
* Built a Python package for weak order comparison, JOIN computation, normalization, and visualization.
